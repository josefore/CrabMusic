About us


This is a site developed by an independent starter programmer, whose name is José Contreras. It was made to test his skills in HTML, CSS, Python and Bootstrap, and also to bring right to your screen the best of his musical knowledge, which is presumed to be one of the finest.

If you want to know more about this developer, you can check the profile on the right and/or visit our Contact us tab.


José Contreras

I am a computer science student and graphic artist. I studied communication sciences at Universidad Iberoamericana, Mexico City and literature at Universidad del Claustro de Sor Juana. I have worked at the radio station Ibero 90.9 as a radio producer, and several other audio companies. In my free times I like to draw and paint both analogue and digital. Currently I'm studying computer science at edX and Harvard University's CS50 courses, and programming in Python and C# in Udemy. Check my profile for more information.
